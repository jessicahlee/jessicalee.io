About Me

Hi there! I’m a product manager and UI/UX designer from New York City. I work on projects in the intelligent buildings space which use the latest artificial intelligence and cybersecurity technologies to improve indoor health. I break down ambitious ideas into action-oriented steps to make intuitive products with robust cybersecurity. I consider myself an advocate for the customer and design for real people, not users. I currently reside in Miami with my three-year-old Shiba Inu, Boxelle.

Education

Cornell University

Master in Information Science • May 2021

A focus area in User Experience, taking project-based courses in Human-Computer Interaction and computer science. I worked on a Bloomberg-sponsored project as a project manager and UI/UX designer to create a data visualization platform for people with colorblindness. I also worked as a Graduate researcher in Social Impact of Farm Networking, supported by the Cornell Institute of Digital Agriculture. At the height of COVID-19, I led a team of graphic designers to create digital assets for Cities' COVID Mitigation and Mapping (C2M2) program based out of Harvard University.

Cornell University

B.S. Degree in Communications • May 2020

A focus area in Persuasion & Social Influence, taking courses on the human part of human-computer interaction. I also worked as a research assistant in the Communication and Collaborative Technologies Lab, researching affiliate stigma among significant others of incarcerated individuals. I also minored in Performance and Media Arts, focusing on theatre and lighting design.
