About Me

Hi there! I’m a product manager and UI/UX designer from New York City. I work on projects in the intelligent buildings space which use the latest artificial intelligence and cybersecurity technologies to improve indoor health. I break down ambitious ideas into action-oriented steps to make intuitive products with robust cybersecuri. I consider myself an advocate for the customer and design for real people, not users. I currently reside in Miami with my three-year-old Shiba Inu, Boxelle.

Education

Cornell University

Master in Information Science • May 2021

A focus area in User Experience, taking project-based courses in Human-Computer Interaction and computer science. I worked on a Bloomberg-sponsored project as a project manager and UI/UX designer to create a data visualization platform for people with colorblindness. I also worked as a Graduate researcher in Social Impact of Farm Networking, supported by the Cornell Institute of Digital Agriculture. Cornell University
